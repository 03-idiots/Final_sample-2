Title: "Data Breach Linked to Social Engineering Tactics"
Date: December 3, 2024

Executive Summary:
A significant data breach has occurred due to social engineering tactics involving Phishing. Attackers gained access to sensitive information by impersonating trusted contacts and establishing Command and Control channels to extract data. This breach highlights the need for vigilance against Exfiltration Techniques that target sensitive databases.

Recommended Mitigations:

Educate employees about the risks of social engineering and Phishing.
Implement data loss prevention (DLP) solutions to monitor sensitive data usage.
Audit logs to identify potential instances of Exfiltration Techniques.
